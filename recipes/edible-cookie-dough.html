Edible Cookie Dough

Description

Edible cookie dough is a safe, delicious solution to cookie dough cravings. Learn how to make this simple dessert today!"

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Place flour in microwave-safe dish and cook for 1 min 15 sec, stirring every 15 secs
  2. Beat sugar and butter in large bowl until creamy. Beat in vanilla extract and salt. Add flour; mix until a crumbly dough forms. Stir in milk until dough is combines, fold in milk chocolate chips and mini chocolate chips.
