Golden Sweet Cornbread

Description

Cornbread is a delicious, simple dish that pairs nicely with a variety of different palletes. Learn how to make cornbread today, and pair it with your favorite soup, salad, or any other dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 Degrees F, grease a 9-inch round cake pan.
  2. Whisk flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, and salt together in large bowl. Add milk, vegetable oil, and egg; continue to whisk, then pour batter into prepared pan.
  3. Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into center of pan will come out clean.
